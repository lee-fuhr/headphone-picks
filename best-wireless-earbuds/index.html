Frequently asked questions

What is true wireless vs wireless earbuds?

True wireless earbuds have no cable between the two earbuds, each bud is completely independent. Older 'wireless' earbuds had a cable connecting left and right buds, which was a compromise. All mainstream earbuds today are true wireless.

How do I get a secure fit with wireless earbuds?

Ear tip size is the most important factor. Most earbuds ship with 3 tip sizes, try each until the earbud sits snugly without pressure. Wing tips or ear hooks help for activity. A proper seal also improves bass and passive isolation significantly.

Can I use wireless earbuds in the shower?

IPX4 and above means sweat and splash resistant. IPX7 means submersible to 1 meter for 30 minutes. Most earbuds in 2026 are at least IPX4 (sweat and rain resistant). True shower use (direct spray) needs IPX5 or above. No earbuds should be submerged in pools or rivers.

Why do my wireless earbuds cut out on one side?

Most often a battery imbalance between buds, or the left-right wireless link being interrupted. Try resetting the earbuds to factory default (usually holding the button 10+ seconds). If one bud consistently dies faster or cuts out, the battery is degrading and may need service.

Do wireless earbuds work without a phone?

Yes if you have a device with Bluetooth, tablets, laptops, some fitness equipment. They won't work without any Bluetooth source device, and features like quick pairing, ANC adjustment, and EQ typically need the manufacturer's app on a phone.
