How we choose our picks

The headphone market has become a dizzying maze of features and competing claims, but the real test is how a pair works in your everyday life. Our buying guides cut through the marketing fluff by gathering insights from owner experiences and published reviews rather than running our own lab tests, so you get curated recommendations that genuinely match your habits and budget. We’re here to help you find the pair that fits—no hype, just honest, well-researched advice.

We don’t pretend to lab-test every model ourselves; instead we read long-term owner reviews and follow reputable testing outlets to see which headphones hold up over months of use, focusing on comfort, app quirks, battery degradation, and support. We then match each pick to how people actually use them, whether for commuting, working out, or late-night listening. Brands like Sony, Bose, Apple, Sennheiser, Jabra, and Anker earn our favour because they have a proven track record for durability and reliable performance.
