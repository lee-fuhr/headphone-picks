How we choose our picks

We don’t rely on quick impressions or flashy launch hype. Instead, we pore over dozens of long‑term owner threads on forums like Reddit and Head‑Fi, track repair and reliability reports from service centers, and collate the findings of professional reviewers who live with these models for weeks. The picks here are the ones that consistently earn praise for build quality, real‑world battery life, and lasting comfort, not just out of the box, but a year or two down the road. We also keep a close eye on what actually breaks. Hinges, headbands, and earpad replacement are common pain points; our top picks either avoid those pitfalls altogether or make repairs straightforward and affordable. If a model has a known firmware bug or a flimsy swivel joint, it gets knocked down, no matter how good it sounds on day one.
