Frequently asked questions

Can budget headphones sound good?

Yes. The gap between a $50 earbud and a $250 earbud has narrowed significantly. Budget models sacrifice premium materials, ecosystem features, and the deepest ANC. but core sound quality and basic ANC are genuinely strong at sub-$80 price points now.

Are cheap ANC earbuds worth it?

Yes, with realistic expectations. Budget ANC cuts low-frequency drone noticeably but won't fully match Sony or Bose. For commuting and office use, budget ANC earbuds make a real difference. For long-haul flights where deep ANC matters most, the premium is usually worth it.

What brand makes the best budget headphones?

Anker's Soundcore line consistently offers the best budget ANC earbuds. For over-ear budget options, Sony's lower tier (WH-CH720N) and Anker Q35 are strong picks. Avoid no-name brands, the quality gap between budget-branded and no-brand is significant.

Do budget earbuds fall out during exercise?

Ear tip fit is the main variable. Most budget earbuds include 3 tip sizes, proper sizing keeps them in place. Earbuds with ear hooks (often found at this price) are more secure for running and lifting. For intense activity, look for wing tips or over-ear hooks.

How long do budget headphones last?

1-3 years with daily use. Battery capacity degrades over hundreds of charge cycles, and budget earbuds typically degrade faster than premium ones. The tradeoff: at $50-70, replacement is less painful than replacing a $250 pair.
