Frequently asked questions

Which headphones have the best noise cancellation?

Over-ear headphones outperform earbuds for deep ANC. the larger ear cup physically blocks more sound before the electronics engage. Sony WH-1000XM5 and Bose QuietComfort 45 are the benchmarks. For earbuds, AirPods Pro 2 and Sony WF-1000XM5 are the leaders.

Does ANC work for all noise types?

ANC works best on steady low-frequency noise: airplane engines, HVAC, traffic rumble. It's less effective on speech, high-pitched sounds, and sudden loud noises. The passive physical seal handles speech isolation; ANC handles the low-frequency drone.

Can ANC cause ear pain or pressure?

Some people experience a slight pressure sensation from ANC. the in-phase sound wave feels mildly pressurizing to some ears. It's not harmful but is uncomfortable for some listeners. Bose tends to produce less of this sensation than Sony; in-ear ANC causes it more than over-ear.

Is transparency mode useful?

Very. Transparency mode passes environmental sound through the headphones so you can hear conversations and announcements without removing them. Useful for commuters, people who run on streets, and office workers who want to stay aware. Quality ranges significantly, AirPods Pro 2 has the most natural-sounding Transparency mode.

Do ANC headphones work without power?

Most still pass audio when the battery dies (wired mode), but ANC stops working. Some cheaper models go completely dead without power. On premium headphones like Sony and Bose, a dead battery means passive listening, quieter but still usable.
